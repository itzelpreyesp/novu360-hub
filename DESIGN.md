# Design System Document

This document outlines the core aesthetic and functional principles of our design system.

## Brand Identity

*   **Color Mode:** Dark
*   **Primary Color:** `#00C2A8` (A vibrant teal, serving as the main brand color for interactive elements and key features.)
*   **Secondary Color:** `#000000` (A deep black, providing strong contrast and depth, used for backgrounds and less prominent elements.)

## Typography

*   **Headlines:** Manrope
*   **Body Text:** Inter
*   **Labels:** Inter

## UI Elements

*   **Roundedness:** Moderate (Corners are moderately rounded, balancing softness with a contemporary feel, providing a default level of visual comfort.)
*   **Spacing:** Normal (A balanced amount of whitespace, ensuring readability and clear separation between elements without feeling too sparse or too dense.)
